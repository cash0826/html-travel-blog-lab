Adventures in Fire Island

Fire Island is a barrier island off the southern shore of Long Island, New York. It is known for its beautiful beaches, historic lighthouse, and vibrant communities.

You can enjoy a variety of activities such as hiking, biking, and swimming

Gallery: Fire Island

Explore the Adventure!

If you're looking for a place to escape the noise and truly feel the world around you, I cannot recommend Fire Island National Seashore enough. It is close to the city, but it feels a million miles away.

For more information, visit the National Park Service website.
